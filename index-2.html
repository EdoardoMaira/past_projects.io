Machine Learning · R

Predicting stock market participation

97% accuracy via 5-fold cross-validation, benchmarking six classifiers on who invests in equities.

Built on the 2022 US Survey of Consumer Finances (23k households): cleaned 356 variables, resolved multicollinearity with GVIF, and compared logistic regression, classification trees, random forest, GBM, SVM (RBF) and kNN.

Financial Econometrics · Bloomberg Terminal

SPX vs Dow Jones: volatility & lead-lag

14 years, ~3,500 daily obs modelled with ARIMA(2,0,2) + GARCH(1,1) under t-distributed innovations.

Sourced from Bloomberg Terminals. Used Granger causality and cointegration tests to map the lead-lag dynamics between the two indices and characterise volatility clustering.

Master's Thesis · Stata + R (geospatial)

Housing bubble dynamics in Denmark

A granular price index across all 98 Danish municipalities, tracing how housing bubbles propagate geographically.

Constructed weighted repeat-sales and hedonic indices — monthly and annual, real and nominal — on Statistics Denmark microdata, with a geospatial layer in R mapping property coordinates over time.

Behavioural Finance · Qualtrics + Stata

Gender priming & financial risk-taking

A randomised experiment measuring how gender-identity salience shifts risk-taking, from primary survey data.

Designed and ran the experiment in Qualtrics, built composite Belief and Risk-Aversion indexes, and estimated the treatment effect with OLS supported by density-plot analysis.
